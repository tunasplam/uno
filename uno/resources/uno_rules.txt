You want to run out of cards before the other players.

RULES
- You may play a card if it matches the COLOR, NUMBER, or SYMBOL of the top card on the discard pile.
- If you cannot play a valid card, you must draw a card.
- You can play cards immediately after drawing

CARD
Number Cards (0–9 in four colors: RGBY)
   - Play matching number or color.

Action Cards:
   - S: Next player loses their turn.
   - R: Reverses direction of play.
   - D: Next player draws two cards.
   - Play matching symbol or color.

Wild Cards:
   - WW: You choose the next color. Playable at any time.
   - WF: You choose the next color. Next player draws four.

CALLING UNO
- When you have 1 card left you should Yell UNO.
- If somebody has 1 card left and you yell uno before they do, they must draw cards.

VALID ACTIONS
Available actions:
  "Play card"   → Attempt to play a specific card from your hand.
  "Draw card"   → Take a card from the draw pile.
  "Do nothing"  → If it is not your turn you will likely do this.
  "Yell UNO"    → Already explained.

CLARIFICATIONS
- You may not choose multiple actions.
